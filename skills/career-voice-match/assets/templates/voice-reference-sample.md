## One-Line Voice Summary

- Clear, measured, and professional; prefers natural wording over polished-sounding filler.

## Tone Sliders

- Formality: medium
- Directness: medium-high
- Warmth: medium
- Self-promotion: low-medium
- Verbosity: low-medium

## Stable Voice Traits

- Prefers straightforward statements over elaborate framing.
- Sounds thoughtful and competent without trying to sound impressive.
- Uses specific verbs and avoids inflated adjectives.

## Preferred Moves

- Open with the point instead of a long setup.
- Keep sentence length moderate and easy to scan.
- Use confidence that is grounded in facts rather than hype.

## Avoid Patterns

- "passionate"
- "dynamic"
- overly ceremonial openings or closings

## Resume Guardrails

- Keep bullets compact and concrete.
- Do not overstate ownership if the original text signals support rather than leadership.

## Cover Letter Guardrails

- Keep interest genuine but understated.
- Let motivation sound specific, not overly enthusiastic or generic.
